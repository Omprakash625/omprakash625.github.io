Shah Rukh Khan

One of the World's Biggest Movie Star

Awards and Achievements

  • 2008 – Newsweek named him one of the 50 most powerful people in the world, ranking him at number 41. He was the only artist and the only movie-star in the list.
  • 2009 – Khan was declared amongst the "500 most influential Muslims in the world" by Georgetown University.
  • 2011 Forbes India Leadership Awards: A special commendation for his contribution to the Media and Entertainment industry.
  • 2013 – Most Popular and Powerful Celebrity in Forbes India Celebrity 100 List and first Indian actor to be featured on the cover of Forbes India Magazine
  • 2013 − Honored as one of the 25 Greatest Global Living Indians by the Honorable President of India Pranab Mukherjee to mark the completion of 25 years of NDTV.
  • 2014 – Recognized as World's Second Richest Actor by Wealth X.
  • 2017 – Honoured at the 60th edition of San Francisco International Film Festival
  • 2018 – ET India-UK Strategic Conclave event: Felicitated with The Economic Times Game Changers of India – Hall of Fame for his unmatched contribution to globalizing Indian cinema.
  • 2019 – Honored for his contribution to the world's film industry at Joy Forum 2019 in Riyadh by The Saudi General Authority for Entertainment.
